How it works

  • 1

    Fax comes in

    CoverMyMeds fax arrives. The system reads it, pulls the patient, medication, payer, and CMM key. Flags duplicates from pharmacy re-sends.

  • 2

    Requirements checked

    Looks up what the payer needs for that medication. Shows what's on file, what's missing. Step therapy, labs, clinical documentation.

  • 3

    Case tracked to resolution

    Kanban board from new through approved. Timelines, deadlines, payer contacts, appeal workflows. Nothing falls through the cracks.
